## **1. Incorrect Username or Password**

One of the most common login issues is entering the wrong account details. Even a small typo can prevent access.

To fix this:

* **Check your username carefully**
* **Make sure your password is typed correctly**
* **Turn off Caps Lock**
* **Avoid extra spaces before or after your login details**
* **Use the password reset option if needed**

## **2. Weak or Unstable Internet Connection**

A slow connection can stop the login page from loading properly. It may also cause the app or website to freeze during sign-in.

Try these steps:

* **Switch to a stronger Wi-Fi or mobile data connection**
* **Restart your router**
* **Close other apps using internet data**
* **Refresh the page or reopen the app**

## **3. Outdated App or Browser**

Using an old app version or outdated browser may cause compatibility issues. This can lead to loading errors, blank pages, or failed login attempts.

To solve this:

* **Update the app to the latest version**
* **Update your browser**
* **Restart your device after updating**
* **Clear old temporary files if problems continue**

## **4. Cache and Cookie Problems**

Stored cache or cookies can sometimes interfere with login pages. If the site does not load correctly, clearing this data may help.

You can try:

* **Clearing browser cache and cookies**
* **Clearing app cache from your phone settings**
* **Restarting the app**
* **Trying a different browser**

## **5. Account Verification Issues**

Some accounts may require extra verification, especially when logging in from a new device or location.

Check if you need to:

* **Verify your mobile number**
* **Confirm your email address**
* **Enter a security code**
* **Complete account verification steps**

## **6. Temporary Maintenance or Server Issues**

Sometimes the problem is not on your device. The platform may be under maintenance or experiencing temporary server issues.

If this happens, wait a few minutes and try again later. You may also check official announcements if available.
